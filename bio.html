bio.txt

introduction

i am an institutional research analyst with three great passions: football, creative arts, and analytical thinking. as i've grown, i've learned to blend these skills in every role i take on a team. i genuinely enjoy helping people and sharing knowledge—this site is an extension of those endeavors.

background & education

born and raised in san diego, i attended westview high school where i played football, basketball, and ran track.

i then went to carleton college, graduating with a bachelor's degree in statistics. i played football collegiately there, serving as a two-time captain. after briefly playing quarterback, i transitioned to wide receiver and punter.

during college, i started my photography and videography business under the name beauhawk digital. i worked primarily in marketing with creative projects in between.

current work & focus

after college, i moved back to san diego and started working at palomar college in 2023 as an institutional research analyst, where i currently work. in this role, i primarily function as a data scientist but have also conducted research and analytical work. i have experience programming in python, sql, and dax.

in 2023, i also began coaching at westview high school, serving as offensive coordinator during the 2023 and 2024 seasons. we reached the cif semifinals both years and came within one score of winning the title in 2024. i have since stepped down from that role.
